What Now?

 

Verify

We will verify your home address, just to make sure that its really you! This prevents people from signing up illegitimate places and ensuring that shelter can actually be provided!

Match

Using the matching criteria you provided, we will pair you with someone who's needs best fit with your home.  We will then send you a follow up email notifying you of your match.

Notify

Don't panic! We are not going to send people to your home without notice! During extreme weather conditions (below freezing or above 95), we will notify you if you have been matched with someone in need of shelter. If you suddenly do not have space at the time, you will have an option to opt out.
